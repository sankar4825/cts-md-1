Community Bulletin

City Council Meeting — June 10

The city council will hold its quarterly meeting on June 10 at City Hall. Residents are encouraged to attend and voice their opinions on the new park development plan that aims to add recreational spaces in the eastern district.

Road Closure Notice

Main Street will be partially closed from June 12–14 for annual maintenance. Alternate routes via Park Avenue and River Road are recommended. Public transport schedules will be adjusted accordingly.

Volunteer Opportunities

Looking for volunteers for the Community Marathon on July 4. Sign up at the community centre or call 1800-CITY-HELP. Volunteers receive free event merchandise and refreshments.

Library Extension Hours

The central library will now remain open until 9 PM on weekdays starting June 1. New digital resource terminals have been installed for residents to access government services online.
